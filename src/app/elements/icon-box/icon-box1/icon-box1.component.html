Vision

To create a world where every individual is equipped with life-saving CPR skills, ensuring that no life is lost due to the lack of timely intervention. Our goal is to make Thrissur the first CPR-certified district in India, expand our reach across Kerala, then nationwide, and ultimately, impact communities globally with accessible and effective CPR training.

Mission

Hands4Heart is committed to empowering individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to perform CPR, recognizing that cardiac emergencies can happen anywhere, at any time. By fostering a culture of preparedness, we aim to train and certify individuals across Thrissur, scale our impact to all of Kerala, and progressively extend our initiative across India and beyond. Our mission is to ensure that life-saving CPR training is accessible to everyone, reinforcing the idea that saving a life starts with a single pair of hands.
