How Our CPR Training Works

Our structured four-step process ensures that you learn, practice, and certify with ease.
  • The first step is simple! Sign up for the training program by completing your registration and making a ₹500 payment. This secures your spot and grants you access to our structured learning modules.
  • After registration, you will receive three comprehensive recorded classes that provide step-by-step guidance on CPR techniques.
  • Once you complete the recorded training, you will be required to take a quiz-based assessment.
  • Upon successfully passing the test, you will receive a CPR certification for a nominal fee of ₹500. This one-year certification proves your expertise in CPR and adds value to your professional and personal skill set.
